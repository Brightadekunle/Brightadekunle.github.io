My Diary

Monday, January 18, 2020


The highlight of what i learnt today is how to communicate effectively as a software developer.
Being a software developer is not just about writing code, there will be times when you may need to explain a particular idea to your team members or a client-facing situation where you have to communicate to an audience about a product. To avoid misunderstanding you have to be articulate in communicating your ideas to people in clear terms. I discovered It is very important and mandatory for every software developer to hone their communication skills.

I also learnt about creating simple web pages, working with html and css files and also loading websites on a public domain using github.

I have the skillset and I believe they are well suited for this position. I love colaborating with people to build softwares and I am excited to learn and get experience in every way possible. I currently write HTML, CSS, Javascript, Nodejs and Python.
I strongly believe I can do this and deliver exceptional results.
